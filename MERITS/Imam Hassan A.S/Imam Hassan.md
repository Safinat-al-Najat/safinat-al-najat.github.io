Hassan Ibn Ali was the eldest grandson of Prophet Muhammad. He is revered as the "[[MERITS/Imam Hassan A.S/Leaders of paradise]]" a title given to him by the Prophet himself.

His importance lies in two main aspects:

First, as a member of the Prophet's household, he was one of the participants in the event of [[MERITS/Ahlulbayt and Panjtan Pak/Mubahala ﷺ|Mubahala ﷺ]] and was deeply loved by his grandfather. 

He is also known for the Peace treaty between Muawiya. He handed over the Caliphate to him over some conditions.

Hassan Ibn Ali was martyred by repeated poisoning. At his deathbed he denied to give the name of the person who poisoned him because he said he don't want to blame any innocent just because of what he thinks.

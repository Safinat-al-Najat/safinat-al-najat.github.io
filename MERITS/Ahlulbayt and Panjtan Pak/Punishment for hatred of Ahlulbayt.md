[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4717](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4717)

If we consider the hadith given above it tells us the significance of Ahlulbayt. Hating them can lead us to Jahanum and following them is the only way to avoid Jahanum.

It seems like their love and hatred is the division of Jannah and Jahanum.



Prophet Muhammad ﷺ is the founder and central figure of Islam. He is the **final and great messenger of Allah** , sent to confirm and complete the teachings of previous prophets like Abraham, Moses, and Jesus.

His importance is absolute for three key reasons:

1. **The Receiver of the Quran:** He received the divine revelations that make up the **Quran**, Islam's holy book, through the Angel Gabriel.
    
2. **The Perfect Example (The Sunnah):** His entire way of life—his actions, sayings, and habits—is known as the **Sunnah**. For Muslims, he is the living embodiment of the Quran's teachings and the perfect model to follow in all aspects of life, from prayer and charity to kindness and business dealings.
    
3. **The Beloved Prophet:** More than just a teacher, he is deeply loved and revered by Muslims as the [[Seal of Prophets]] the one who delivered God's final message to humanity.

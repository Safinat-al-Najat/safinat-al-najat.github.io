# ISA GREETING MUHAMMAD ﷺ

## Mustadrak Al Hakim 4169

![[image-581.webp|418x601]]
![[image-580.webp|418x601]]

> I heard Abu Hurairah saying:
> 
> The Messenger of Allah ﷺ said: 'Isa (Jesus), the son of Maryam (Mary), will surely descend as a just judge and an equitable leader. He will certainly travel through a mountain pass as a pilgrim performing _Hajj_ or _'Umrah_, or combining both of them. And he will definitely come to my grave until he greets me with peace, and I will surely return his greeting.'
> 
> Abu Hurairah would say: 'O son of my brother, if you see him, tell him: Abu Hurairah sends his greetings of peace to you.'"

> **The Scholar's Verdict:**  
> "This is a Hadith with a _Sahih_ (authentic) chain of narrators, though they [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] did not record it with this exact wording."

---

>   **4162 -** "He [Al-Dhahabi] said in _Al-Talkhis_: _Sahih_ (Authentic)."

## Musnad Abi Ya'ala 6584

![[image-583.webp|418x601]]![[image-582.webp|418x601]]
> I _heard the Messenger of Allah ﷺ say:_
> 
> 'By the One in Whose hand is the soul of Abu al-Qasim, **Isa ibn Maryam shall surely descend** as a just leader and an equitable judge. He shall surely break the cross, slaughter the swine, reconcile mutual differences, and eliminate malice. Wealth shall be offered to him, but no one will accept it. **Then, if he were to stand at my grave and say: "Ya Muhammad!" (O Muhammad), I would surely respond to him.**'"

>  **Authenticity Verdict:** It begins with **(1) إسناده صحيح** (_"Its chain of narration is Sahih/authentic"_). It clarifies that the narrator Abu Sakhr is Humayd ibn Ziyad, a known trustworthy transmitter.

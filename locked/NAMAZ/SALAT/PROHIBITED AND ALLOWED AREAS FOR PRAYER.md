Ibn Abbas, may Allah be pleased with him said:

**Core Text:**

> The Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him and his family, said:  
> "I have been given five things which no one was given before me: the earth has been made for me a place of prayer (_masjid_) and a means of purification (_tahur_), I have been aided by fear [cast into the hearts of enemies], spoils of war have been made lawful for me, I have been given all-comprehensive speech (_jawami' al-kalim_), and I have been given [the right of] intercession (_shafa'ah_)."


So, whole land is allowed for prayer but...

Imam Abu ‘Abd Allah (Ja'far al-Sadiq, peace be upon him) [reported via an unnamed transmitter]

**Core Text:**

> "There are ten places in which prayer is not to be performed: mud, water, the bathhouse, graves, the pathways of the road, anthills, camel resting-places, watercourses, salt flats, and snow."

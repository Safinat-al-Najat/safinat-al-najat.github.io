Sulayman bin Khalid (reporting from Imam Ja'far al-Sadiq, peace be upon him)

**Core Text:**

> Sulayman bin Khalid said to al-Sadiq, peace be upon him: "May I be ransomed for you! Inform me about the obligations that Allah, the Mighty and Majestic, has imposed upon the servants, what are they?"
> 
> He said: "The testimony that there is no god but Allah, and that Muhammad is the Messenger of Allah ﷺ, establishing the five prayers, paying the zakat, performing the pilgrimage to the House, fasting the month of Ramadan, and the Wilayah (divinely ordained leadership). Therefore, whoever establishes them, acts with moderation, strives to do right, and avoids every forbidden matter, will enter Paradise."

As Allah says in [Quran 4:80](https://quran.com/4/80):

"**He who obeys the Messenger has obeyed Allah...**"

and also in [Quran 3:31](https://quran.com/3/31)

"**Say, (O Muhammad), 'If you should love Allah, then follow me, (so) Allah will love you and forgive you your sins.**"

Since the Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) is Allah's most beloved creation, hating him contradicts this fundamental requirement of faith.

So if we hate Ali we hate Aqa Muhammad ﷺ and if we hate Aqa Muhammad ﷺ then we hate Allah.

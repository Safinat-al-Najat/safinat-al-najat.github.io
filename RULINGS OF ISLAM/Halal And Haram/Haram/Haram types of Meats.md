[Quran 2:173](https://quran.com/2/173)

Carrion is already dead meat that might have died of any disease or any injury.
It is haram to consume blood.
Swine (pig) is also haram.
Any animal not slaughtered in name of Allah.

But Allah provides ease for Muslims that if it is necessary to eat to save their live then they can eat haram but only to save their lives.

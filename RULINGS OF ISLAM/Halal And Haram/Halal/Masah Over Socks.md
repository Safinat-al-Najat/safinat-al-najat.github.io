Above hadith tells about the Leather socks. Which are also called Khuff or Khuffain.

![[image-16.png]]

Above tells about simple socks not too thin but also thick that water doesn't enters inside. It's ok if you feel a little cold after wiping over socks but water shouldn't enter.

It is to be noted that to perform this act you should have worn socks after Wudu and then if wudu breaks while wearing socks you can wipe over it.

If wudu breaks while you are not wearing then you have to perform wudu without wearing it.

For non-travelers, 24 hours are allowed while for travelers 3 days are allowed, meeting the above condition.

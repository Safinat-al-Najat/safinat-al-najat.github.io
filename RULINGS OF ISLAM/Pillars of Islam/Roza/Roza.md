> **Abu Umamah al-Bahili, may Allah be pleased with him, narrated to me, saying: I heard the Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, say:**
> 
> 'While I was sleeping, two men came to me and took me by my arms. They brought me to a steep, rugged mountain and said to me: "Climb." I replied: "I cannot do it." They said: "We will make it easy for you." So I climbed until, when I was in the middle of the mountain, I suddenly heard severe, loud noises. I asked: "What are these sounds?" They answered: "This is the howling of the people of the Hellfire." Then I was taken further, and I saw people suspended by their achilles tendons, with their jaw corners torn and bleeding profusely. I asked: "Who are these?" He replied: "These are those who break their fast before the prescribed time for its completion."'**


> **[Al-Hakim's evaluation]: This is a sahih (authentic) hadith according to the criteria of Muslim, though the two Shaikhs [al-Bukhari and Muslim] did not record it.**

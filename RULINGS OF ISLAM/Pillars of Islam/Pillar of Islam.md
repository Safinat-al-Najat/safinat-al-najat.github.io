## The Five Pillars — Core Obligations

These are the most fundamental obligations in Islam:

**Shahada** — declaration of faith: "There is no god but Allah, and Muhammad is His messenger."

**Salah** — the five daily prayers, performed at fixed times throughout the day.

**Zakat** — obligatory almsgiving, a fixed portion of wealth given to those in need annually.

**Sawm** — fasting during the month of Ramadan, abstaining from food, drink, and sin from dawn to sunset.

**Hajj** — pilgrimage to Makkah, obligatory once in a lifetime for those physically and financially capable.

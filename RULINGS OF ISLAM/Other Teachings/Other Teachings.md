
Other teachings in Islam refer to all the guidance found in the Quran, the Sunnah of the Aqa Muhammad ﷺ. This includes moral conduct, social responsibilities, spiritual purification, the rights of others, and how a Muslim should behave in every aspect of daily life. Essentially it covers everything that shapes a Muslim into a person of good character, justice, and genuine closeness to Allah — making Islam not just a set of rituals but a complete and comprehensive way of life.

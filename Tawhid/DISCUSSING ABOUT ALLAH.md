Abu Jafar al-Baqir  said:  

“Discuss the creation of Allah, but refrain from discussing about Allah. Verily, discussing about Allah causes nothing but confusion.”


[Al Tawhid Book 2 Ch 67 H#1](https://thaqalayn.net/hadith/14/2/67/1)

And It is a fact indeed, because our brains, that are created by Allah cannot ever comprehend what Allah is. It will always cause confusion. As said in the following narration.

![[image-141.webp|418x601]]![[image-139.webp|418x601]]![[image-140.webp|418x601]]
 I asked Aba Ja’far the Second عليه السلام about Tawhid saying:

Should I imagine a thing (when conceiving of God)? He said: Yes, (something) neither cognisable nor delimited. Whatever your imagination falls upon is different than He. Nothing is like Him and imaginations do not reach Him. How can imaginations reach Him when He is other than what can be cognised and other than what is represented (image formed) in the imaginations? It is imagined something neither cognisable nor delimited.


[Mujam Al Ahadith Al Mutbara Book 4 Ch 3 H#8](https://thaqalayn.net/hadith/9/4/3/8)

There are various narration in the same chapter of the book.

Abu Jafar al-Baqir  said:  
  
“Mention Allah’s Greatness as you wish, but do not discuss His Essence, as He is greater than anything you may mention.”


[Al Tawhid Book 2 Ch 67 H#3](https://thaqalayn.net/hadith/14/2/67/3)

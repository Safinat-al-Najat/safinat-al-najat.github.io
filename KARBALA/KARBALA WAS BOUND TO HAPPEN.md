> 3 – My father, may Allah be pleased with him, narrated to us, saying: Habib bin al-Husayn al-Taghlibi narrated to us, saying: Abbad bin Ya'qub narrated to us, from 'Amr bin Thabit, from Abu al-Jaroud, from Abu Ja'far, peace be upon him, who said:
> 
> The Prophet, peace be upon him and his family, was in the house of Umm Salama, may Allah be pleased with her, and he said to her: "Do not let anyone enter upon me." Then al-Husayn, peace be upon him, came while he was a child, and she could not prevent him from entering until he reached the Prophet, peace be upon him and his family.
> 
> Umm Salama entered right after him and found al-Husayn on his chest, while the Prophet, peace be upon him and his family, was weeping, holding something in his hand and turning it over. The Prophet, peace be upon him and his family, said: "O Umm Salama, Gabriel is informing me that this one will be killed, and this is the soil upon which he will be killed. Keep it with you, and when it turns into blood, then my beloved has been killed."
> 
> Umm Salama said: "O Messenger of Allah, ask Allah to avert that from him." He said: "I have done so, but Allah, the Almighty and Sublime, revealed to me: 'Verily, he has a degree (station) that no other creation shall attain, and he has Shia (followers) who will intercede and their intercession will be accepted, and verily, the Mahdi is from his offspring.' So blessed is he who is among the companions of al-Husayn, and his Shia, by Allah, are the successful ones on the Day of Resurrection."


[Al Amali by Sheikh Al Saduq](https://thaqalayn.net/hadith/29/1/29/3)

Umm e Salma Passed away after karbala as it is also confirmed by Dhahabi (Renowned Sunni Scholar)

![[image-517.webp|418x601]]![[image-516.webp|418x601]]
>"She was the last of the Mothers of the Believers to pass away. She lived a long life until she received the news of the killing of al-Husayn, the martyr, and she was deeply devastated by it, fainted, and grieved for him immensely. She did not remain [alive] long after him before she passed away to Allah."

> 15116 – And from Umm Salama, she said: "The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him and his family, was sitting one day in my house, and he said: 'Do not let anyone enter upon me.' I waited, then al-Husayn entered, and I heard the sobbing of the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him and his family, weeping. I looked in, and there was Husayn in his lap, and the Prophet, peace be upon him and his family, was wiping his forehead and weeping.
> 
> I said: 'By Allah, I did not know when he entered.' He said: 'Gabriel, peace be upon him, was with us in the house, and he said: Do you love him? I said: As for worldly matters, yes. He said: Verily, your nation will kill this one in a land called Karbala.'
> 
> So Gabriel took some of its soil and showed it to the Prophet, peace be upon him and his family. When they surrounded Husayn at the time he was killed, he asked: 'What is the name of this land?' They said: 'Karbala.' He said: 'Allah and His Messenger spoke the truth; a land of distress and affliction (Karb wa Bala).'"

> (1391) Ibrahim bin Abdullah narrated to us, saying: Hajjaj narrated to us, saying: Hammad narrated to us, from Aban, from Shahr bin Hawshab, from Umm Salama, who said: "Gabriel, peace be upon him, was with the Prophet, peace be upon him and his family, and al-Husayn was with me, so he wept. I left him, and he drew near to the Prophet, peace be upon him and his family. Gabriel said: 'Do you love him, O Muhammad?' He replied: 'Yes.' Gabriel said: 'Verily, your nation will kill him, and if you wish, I can show you some of the soil of the land in which he will be killed.' So he showed it to him, and behold, it was a land called Karbala."

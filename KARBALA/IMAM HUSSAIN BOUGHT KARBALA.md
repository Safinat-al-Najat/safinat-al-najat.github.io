> **12092] 7 –** And from the aforementioned book: It is narrated that Al-Husayn (peace be upon him) purchased the surrounding lands where his grave is located from the people of Nineveh and Al-Ghadiriyyah for sixty thousand dirhams. He then gave it back to them as a charity, on the condition that they guide visitors to his grave and host whoever visits him for three days.

**Mustadrak Al Wasail 12092**

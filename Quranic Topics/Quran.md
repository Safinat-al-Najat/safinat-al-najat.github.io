
The Quran is the central, sacred scripture of Islam, believed by Muslims to be the literal, unchanged word of Allah revealed to Prophet Muhammad ﷺ through the angel Gabriel over 23 years. It serves as the ultimate guide for faith, morality, law, and daily life, emphasizing the oneness of Allah.

Abu Ayyub al-Ansari, may Allah be pleased with him

**Core Text:**

> "I heard the Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, saying to Ali ibn Abi Talib: 'You will fight the oath-breakers (_al-Nakithin_), the unjust (_al-Qasitin_), and the renegades (_al-Mariqin_) at the pathways, at al-Nahrawan, and at the mountain peaks.' Abu Ayyub said: I asked, 'O Messenger of Allah, with whom will those groups be fought?' He replied, 'With Ali ibn Abi Talib.'"

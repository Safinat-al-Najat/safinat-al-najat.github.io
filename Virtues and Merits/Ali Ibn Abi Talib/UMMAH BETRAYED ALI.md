Ali, may Allah be pleased with him, narrated:

> "Indeed, among the covenants that the Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, made with me is that the nation will surely betray you after me."
> 
> **Author's Comment:** This is a hadith with an authentic chain of transmission (sahih al-isnad), but they [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] did not record it.

Ali narrated:

> "The Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, said to me: 'Indeed, the nation will betray you after me, and you will live upon my religion, and you will be killed upon my Sunnah. Whoever loves you loves me, and whoever hates you hates me. And indeed, this—meaning his beard—will be dyed from this—meaning his head.'"

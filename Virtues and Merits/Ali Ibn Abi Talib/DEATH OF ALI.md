**Ibn Shihab says:**  
"I came from Damascus with the intention of Jihad. When I arrived to offer my salutations to Abdul Malik, he was at that time near the floor being built for the minaret, present inside a dome with two rows of people seated beneath him. I offered my greetings and sat down.

He said to me, 'O Ibn Shihab, do you know what the state of Bayt al-Maqdis (Jerusalem) was on the day Ali (Radi Allahu Anhu) was martyred?'

I replied, 'Yes.'

He signaled me to come closer, so I passed behind the people and reached the back of the dome. He turned his face toward me and said with great kindness, 'What was the state?'

I replied, 'In Bayt al-Maqdis, every stone that was lifted had blood beneath it.'

He said, 'In the world today, no one knows this besides you and me. (But) do not mention this to anyone now.'

Consequently, I did not tell this to anyone until the death of Abdul Malik."

[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4591](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4591)

"Imam Zuhri narrates the statement of Hazrat Asma Al-Ansariya that on the night Hazrat Ali (may Allah be pleased with him) was martyred, every stone lifted in Aelia (Jerusalem) had fresh blood beneath it. 

Imam Hakim states: What was the age of the Commander of the Faithful, Hazrat Ali (may Allah be pleased with him), at the time of his martyrdom? There are various narrations regarding this."

[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4694](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4694)

Blood was found under stones after death of Ali.

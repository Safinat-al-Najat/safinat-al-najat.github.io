Ali, may Allah be pleased with him

**Core Text:**

> The Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, said:  
> "May Allah have mercy upon Ali. O Allah, turn the truth with him wherever he turns."

**Author's Comment:**

> "This is an authentic (_sahih_) hadith according to the criteria of Muslim, but they [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] did not record it."

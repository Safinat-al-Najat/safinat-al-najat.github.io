Umm Salamah, may Allah be pleased with her (reported by Abu Abdillah al-Jadali)

**Core Text:**

> Abu Abdillah al-Jadali said: "I performed the pilgrimage when I was a young boy, and I passed by Medina. The people were moving together as one large group, so I followed them. They entered upon Umm Salamah, the wife of the Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him.
> 
> I heard her call out: 'O Shabib bin Rib'i!' A harsh and coarse man replied to her: 'At your service, O mother!' She said: 'Is the Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, being insulted in your gathering?' He asked: 'How could that be?' She replied: '[By insulting] Ali ibn Abi Talib.' He said: 'Indeed, we say certain things merely seeking the fleeting gains of this world.' She said: 'For I surely heard the Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, say: _Whosoever insults Ali has insulted me, and whosoever insults me has insulted Allah the Almighty._'"


[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4616](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4616)

Cursing Ali is cursing equivalent to cursing Muhammad ﷺ.

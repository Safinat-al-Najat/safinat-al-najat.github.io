Ibn Abbas (reporting from Ali, may Allah be pleased with them both)

**Core Text:**

> "Ali used to say during the lifetime of the Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him: 'Indeed Allah says: _“If he dies or is killed, will you turn back on your heels?”_ [Quran 3:144] By Allah, we shall not turn back on our heels after Allah has guided us! By Allah, if he dies or is killed, I will surely fight for what he fought for until I die. By Allah, I am his brother, his ally, his paternal cousin, and the inheritor of his knowledge; so who is more entitled to him than me?'"


[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4635](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4635)

Ali will fight for Aqa Muhammad ﷺ till death.

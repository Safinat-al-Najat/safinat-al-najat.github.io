"Hazrat Abdullah bin Abbas (may Allah be pleased with him) said: The Messenger of Allah (peace and blessings of Allah be upon him) said: I am the city of knowledge and Ali is its gate, so whoever wants to enter the city should enter through the gate."

Hussain bin Fahm said: Abu Salat al-Hurawi narrated this hadith to us on the authority of Abu Muawiyah. Imam Hakim said: Whoever is interested in this knowledge should know that Hussain bin Fahm bin Abdur Rahman is trustworthy, trustworthy and a Hafiz.

The following hadith narrated by Sufyan al-Thawri with a sahih chain of transmission is a witness to the aforementioned hadith."

[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4638](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4638)

Ali is the gate of the city of knowledge and Aqa Muhammad ﷺ is the city of knowledge.

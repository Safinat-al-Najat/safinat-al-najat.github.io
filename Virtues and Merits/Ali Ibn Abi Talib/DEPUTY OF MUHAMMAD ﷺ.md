"Hazrat Abdullah bin Abbas (RA) narrates: The Prophet (ﷺ) said: Who among you will be my friend in this world and the Hereafter? He asked each person whether he would make me his friend in this world and the Hereafter? Everyone answered in the negative. When Hazrat Ali (RA) said: I will make you my friend in this world and the Hereafter, he said: (That is correct) you are my friend in this world and the Hereafter." ******

This hadith has a sahih isnaad, but Imam Bukhari (RA) and Imam Muslim (RA) did not narrate it."

[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4655](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4655)

Ali is the deputy of Muhammad ﷺ.

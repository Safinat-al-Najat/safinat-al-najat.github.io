This shows how much devotion Ali had for Aqa Muhammad ﷺ.

![[image-53.webp|418x601]]

"Hazrat Abu Huraira (RA) narrates that Hazrat Umar bin Khattab (RA) said: 'Hazrat Ali (RA) was granted three virtues; if I possessed even one of them, it would be dearer to me than red camels.' He was asked: 'O Leader of the Faithful! What are those virtues?' He replied:

1. **His marriage to the daughter of the Messenger of Allah (SAW).**
2. **His residence in the mosque with the Messenger of Allah (SAW)**, where everything that was lawful for the Prophet (SAW) was also lawful for him.
3. **The standard (flag) given to him on the day of the Battle of Khaybar.**

This Hadith is Sahih (authentic) in its chain of narration, though it was not recorded by the Shaykhayn (Imam Bukhari and Imam Muslim)." (Translated)

[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4632](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4632)

So, Ali did the same Niyah for Ihram as Aqa Muhammad ﷺ.
Ali had three distinctions.

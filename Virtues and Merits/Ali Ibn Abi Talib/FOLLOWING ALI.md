Abu Dharr, may Allah be pleased with him

**Core Text:**

> The Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, said to Ali ibn Abi Talib, may Allah be pleased with him:  
> "Whoever obeys me has obeyed Allah, and whoever disobeys me has disobeyed Allah. And whoever obeys you has obeyed me, and whoever disobeys you has disobeyed me."

**Author's Comment:**

> "This is a hadith with an authentic chain of transmission (_sahih al-isnad_), but they [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] did not record it."

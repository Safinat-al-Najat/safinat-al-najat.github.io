"Hazrat Malik bin Dinar (may Allah be pleased with him) narrates: I asked Sa'id bin Jubayr (may Allah be pleased with him): 'O Abu Abdullah! Who was the standard-bearer (Alam-dar) of the Messenger of Allah ﷺ?' (Malik bin Dinar says:) Sa'id looked at me and said: 'You are very weak-hearted.' This remark did not sit well with me, and I complained about him to my fellow reciters (Qurra). I told them: 'I asked Sa'id: "Who was the standard-bearer of the Messenger of Allah ﷺ?" He looked at me and said: "You are a very weak-hearted person."'

People told me: 'You asked him this question while he is in a state of fear due to Hajjaj (ibn Yusuf), because of whom he is currently in hiding. Now, you go back and ask him again.' I went back and asked him the same question. He replied: **'The standard-bearer of the Messenger of Allah ﷺ was Hazrat Ali (may Allah be pleased with him).'** I also heard exactly this from Hazrat Abdullah bin Abbas (may Allah be pleased with them).

(The text continues with a scholarly note:) This Hadith is _Sahih al-Isnad_ (has a sound chain of narration), but Imam Bukhari and Imam Muslim (may Allah have mercy on them) have not recorded it. There is also a supporting narration by Zanfal al-Urfi for this Hadith, but it is very lengthy, so I have not included it here."


[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4665](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4665)

To make it easier to understand, in this hadith Malik bin Dinar asked Said Ibn Jubayr that who was flag bearer of  in every battle and he reply: "Ali".

So, Ali was the flag bearer of Muhammad ﷺ.

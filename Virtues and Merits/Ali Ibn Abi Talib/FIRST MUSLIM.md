Zayd bin Arqam, may Allah be pleased with him

**Core Text:**

> "The first to accept Islam with the Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, was Ali ibn Abi Talib, may Allah be pleased with him."

**Author's Comment:**

> "This is a hadith with an authentic chain of transmission (_sahih al-isnad_). The disagreement regarding this matter is only that Abu Bakr al-Siddiq, may Allah be pleased with him, was the first of the adult men to accept Islam, whereas Ali ibn Abi Talib's acceptance of Islam preceded his reaching the age of puberty."


[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4663](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4633)

Ali was first to embrace Islam.

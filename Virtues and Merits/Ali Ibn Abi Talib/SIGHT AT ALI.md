Scan 1 (Page 280 - Hadith 4681)

**Narrated by Imran bin Husayn:**

> The Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, said:  
> "Looking at Ali is an act of worship."
> 
> **Author's Comment:** This is a hadith with an authentic chain of transmission (_sahih al-isnad_), and its supporting narrations from Abdullah bin Mas'ud are authentic.

---

Scan 2 (Page 281 - Hadith 4682 & 4683)

**Narrated by Abdullah bin Mas'ud:**

> The Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, said:  
> "Looking at the face of Ali is an act of worship."

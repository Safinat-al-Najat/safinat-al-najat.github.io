Ibn Abbas, may Allah be pleased with them both

**Core Text:**

> The Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, said to Ali:  
> "Indeed, you will encounter hardship/trials after me." Ali asked: "[Will it be] while my religion is safe?" He [the Prophet] replied: "[Yes], while your religion is safe."

**Author's Comment:**

> "This is an authentic (_sahih_) hadith according to the criteria of the two Sheikhs [Al-Bukhari and Muslim], but they did not record it."

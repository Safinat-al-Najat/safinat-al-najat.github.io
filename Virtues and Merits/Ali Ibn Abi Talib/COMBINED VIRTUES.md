[Jami at Tirmidhi 3724](https://sunnah.com/tirmidhi:3724)

Main points are:

1. Ali is like Harun PBUH of Muhammad ﷺ as Harun PBUH was for Moses PBUH. But there won't be any Prophet after Muhammad ﷺ.
2. Ali was the one who got banner of victory on the day of Khaibar
3. Ali was included in the family of Muhammad ﷺ along with Bibi Fatima, Hassan and Hussain.

 **A number of our people has narrated from Ahmad ibn Muhammad from al-Hajjal from Tha‘labah that Zurarah who has said that he heard abu Ja‘far and abu ‘Abdallah  say the following. “Allah, the Most Holy, the Most High has made the Holy Prophet in charge of the affairs of His creatures to see how their obedience is. He then recited this verse of Holy Quran, “. .what the Messenger (of Allah) you, you must take (obey) and what he prohibits you, you must desist from.**

[Al Kafi Book 4 Ch 52 H#3](https://thaqalayn.net/hadith/1/4/52/3)

The above hadith tells us how significant our Aqa Muhammad ﷺ is in this world. We must obey what he says and must forbid ourselves from doing what he prohibits.

The Quranic Verse used in the hadith is [Quran 59:7](https://quran.com/59/7)

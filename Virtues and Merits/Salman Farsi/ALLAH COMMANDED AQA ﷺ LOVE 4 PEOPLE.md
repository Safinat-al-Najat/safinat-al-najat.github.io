Buraydah (from his father)

**Core Text:**

> The Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him and his family, said:  
> "Indeed, Allah has commanded me to love four of my companions, and He informed me that He loves them. We said: 'Who are they, O Messenger of Allah? For there is none among us except that he wishes to be one of them.' He, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him and his family, said: 'Behold, indeed Ali is among them'—saying it three times—'and Al-Miqdad bin al-Aswad, Abu Dharr al-Ghifari, and Salman al-Farsi.'"


[Al Amali Book 15 Ch 2 H#1](https://thaqalayn.net/chapter/13/15/2)

![[image-172.webp|418x601]]![[image-170.webp|418x601]]![[image-171.webp|418x601]]
> The Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, said:  
> "Indeed, Allah has commanded me to love four of my companions, and He informed me that He loves them." We said: "Who are they, O Messenger of Allah? For every one of us would love to be among them." He said: "Behold, indeed Ali is among them," then he remained silent. Then he said: "Behold, indeed Ali is among them," then he remained silent.

**Author's Comment:**

> "This is an authentic (_sahih_) hadith according to the criteria of Muslim, but they [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] did not record it."

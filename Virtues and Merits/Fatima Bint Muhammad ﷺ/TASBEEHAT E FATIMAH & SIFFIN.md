"Hazrat Ali (Radi Allahu Anhu) narrates: The Messenger of Allah (Sallallahu Alayhi Wasallam) came to us and sat between me and Fatima. We asked: 'When we go to bed (to sleep), what should we recite?'

He replied: 'Recite **SubhanAllah** 33 times, **Alhamdulillah** 33 times, and **Allahu Akbar** 34 times.'

Hazrat Ali (Radi Allahu Anhu) says: 'By Allah! After that, I never missed these glorifications (Tasbihat).' A man who held some grievance against him in his heart asked: 'Not even on the night of Siffin?' Hazrat Ali (Radi Allahu Anhu) replied: 'Yes, I did not leave them even on the night of Siffin.'

**Note:** This Hadith is authentic (Sahih) according to the standards of Imam Bukhari (RA) and Imam Muslim (RA), but they did not include it (in their primary collections)."

Authentic as per Bukhari and Muslim

[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4724](https://al-hadees.com/mustadrak/4724)

Hadith shows that how much the tasbeeh was loved by Mola Ali that even during the times of battle he never left these recitations.

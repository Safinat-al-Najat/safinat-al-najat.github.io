"Hazrat Musawwar bin Makhrama narrates that the Messenger of Allah (peace be upon him) said: "Fatima is a part of my liver (flesh and blood), what pleases her pleases me, and what hurts her hurts me."

This hadith has a sound chain of narration (Sahih al-Isnad), but Imam Bukhari and Imam Muslim did not include it.

[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4743](https://al-hadees.com/mustadrak/4743)

This hadith shows the love of  Muhammad ﷺ for his great daughter. Whatever please Bibi Fatima pleases  Muhammad ﷺ and whatever hurts Bibi Fatima hurts  Muhammad ﷺ.

Bibi Fatima is a part of  Muhammad ﷺ indeed.

**Main Narrator:** Ali, may Allah be pleased with him

**Core Text:**

> Ali, may Allah be pleased with him, said: "When Hasan was born, I named him Hamzah. Then when Husayn was born, I named him after his paternal uncle, Ja'far." He said: "Then the Messenger of Allah ﷺ called me and said: 'Indeed, I have been commanded to change the names of these two.' So I said: 'Allah and His Messenger know best.' Thus, he named them Hasan and Husayn."

**Author's Comment:**

> "[Shu'ayb al-Arna'ut said:] Its chain of transmission is good (_hasan_)."

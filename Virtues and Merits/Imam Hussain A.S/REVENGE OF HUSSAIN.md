Ibn Abbas, may Allah be pleased with them both

**Core Text:**

> The Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, said:  
> "Allah the Almighty revealed to Muhammad ﷺ: 'Indeed, I killed seventy thousand for the blood of John the son of Zechariah (Yahya bin Zakariyya), and I will surely kill seventy thousand and seventy thousand for the blood of your daughter's son (Al-Husayn).'"
> 
> This is the wording of the hadith according to Al-Shafi'i. In the narration of Al-Qadi Abu Bakr bin Kamil, it reads: "Indeed, I killed for the blood of John the son of Zechariah, and I will surely kill for the blood of your daughter's son."

**Author's Comment:**

> "This is a hadith with an authentic chain of transmission (_sahih al-isnad_), but they [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] did not record it."

Hussain Ibn Ali was the younger grandson of Prophet Muhammad. His importance resonates throughout the entire Islamic world. The Prophet had declared both Hassan and Hussain as [[Virtues and Merits/Imam Hassan A.S/LEADERS OF JANNAH]]

His importance stems almost entirely from a single, defining event: the tragedy of Karbala.

After the death of his brother Hassan, Hussain was the Imam. For years, he lived under the rule of Muawiya, honoring the peace treaty his brother had made. But when Muawiya died and his son Yazid took power, everything changed. Yazid was a tyrant, known for his injustice and impiety. He demanded Hussain's allegiance, which would have meant legitimizing Yazid's corrupt rule.

He refused allegiance and it resulted in the tragedy of Karbala.

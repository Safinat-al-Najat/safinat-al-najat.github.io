**Main Narrator:** Ya'la bin Murrah

**Core Text:**

> The Messenger of Allah ﷺ said: "Husayn is from me and I am from Husayn. May Allah love whoever loves Husayn. Husayn is a sibt among asbat."

**Author's Comment:**

> [Abu Isa said:] This hadith is good (_hasan_). We only know of it from the hadith of Abdullah bin Uthman bin Khuthaym. And more than one narrator has reported it from Abdullah bin Uthman bin Khuthaym.

**Narrated Zaid bin Arqam:**

**that the Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) said to 'Ali, Bibi Fatima, Al-Hasan and Al-Husain: "I am at war with whoever makes war with you, and peace for whoever makes peace with you."**

[Jami at Tirmidhi 3870](https://sunnah.com/tirmidhi:3870)
[Sahih Ibn Hibban 6977](https://hadithanswers.com/nabi-sallallahu-alayhi-wa-sallam-is-at-peace-with-those-who-are-at-peace-with-his-family-members/)

So, whoever is at war with Panjtan is at war with Aqa ﷺ.

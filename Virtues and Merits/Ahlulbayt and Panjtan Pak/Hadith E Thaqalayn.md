![[image-104.png]]

(21470) Its chain of narration (isnad) is _hasan_ (good), due to Shareek and due to Al-Qasim bin Hassan Al-Amiri. Al-Ijli, Ibn Hibban, and Ibn Shaheen authenticated him, while Al-Bukhari and others declared him unknown. The hadith was mentioned previously, close to this one, in (11046) and (11073).

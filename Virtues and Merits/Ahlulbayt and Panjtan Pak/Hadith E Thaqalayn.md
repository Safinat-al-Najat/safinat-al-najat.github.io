Given Hadith tells the strong connection between Quran and Ahlulbayt that they wont even separate from each other and will meet with Aqa Muhammad ﷺ at Kauthar.

So, one can derive that following Ahlulbayt is actually following Quran.

Hadith 2457

**The Prophetic Saying:**

> "Indeed, I am leaving among you two successors (_khalifatayn_): the Book of Allah—a rope extended between heaven and earth—and my progeny, the members of my household (_Ahl al-Bayt_). And indeed, they will never separate until they return to me at the Pond."

**Grading and Sources:**

- **Authentic (Sahih)**
- Narrated by Ahmad (`حم`) and Al-Tabarani (`طب`) from **Zayd ibn Thabit**.
- References: _Al-Rawd al-Nadir_ 977, 978.

---

Hadith 2458

**The Prophetic Saying:**

> "Indeed, I am leaving among you that which if you hold fast to, you will never go astray after me; one of them is greater than the other: the Book of Allah—a rope extended from heaven to earth—and my progeny, the members of my household (_Ahl al-Bayt_). And they will never separate until they return to me at the Pond. So see how you succeed me regarding them."

**Grading and Sources:**

- **Authentic (Sahih)**
- Narrated by Al-Tirmidhi (`ت`) from **Zayd ibn Arqam**.
- References: _Al-Rawd al-Nadir_ 977, _Al-Mishkat_ 6144.

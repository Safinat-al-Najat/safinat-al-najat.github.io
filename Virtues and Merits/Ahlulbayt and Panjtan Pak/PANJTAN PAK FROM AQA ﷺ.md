The Prophet (s) met Ali, Fatima, al-Hasan, and al-Husayn (a.s), and all of them were saying, “I am the most beloved to the Messenger of Allah (s).” So, he (s) took Fatima in front of him, Ali to his back, al-Hasan to his right, and al-Husayn to his left. Then, he (s) said: You are from me, and I am from you."

[Al Amali Book 1 Ch 4 H#2](https://thaqalayn.net/hadith/29/1/4/2)

[Sahih Bukhari 3371](https://sunnah.com/bukhari:3371) 

![[image-293.webp|418x601]]![[image-289.webp|418x601]]![[image-290.webp|418x601]]

Ibn Abbas, may Allah be pleased with them both

**Core Text:**

> "The Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, used to seek protection for Al-Hasan and Al-Husayn, saying: 'I seek protection for you both with the perfect words of Allah from every devil and poisonous pest, and from every accusing/evil eye.' Then he would say: 'This is how Abraham used to seek protection for his two sons, Ishmael and Isaac.'"

**Author's Comment:**

> "This is an authentic (_sahih_) hadith according to the criteria of the two Sheikhs [Al-Bukhari and Muslim], but they did not record it."

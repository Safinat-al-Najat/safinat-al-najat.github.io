**Core Text:**

> "We went out with the Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, and we were invited to a meal. Suddenly, Husayn was playing in the street. The Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, rushed ahead of the people, then stretched out his hands, and the boy began running here and there, while the Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, made him laugh until he caught him. Then he placed one of his hands under his chin and the other on his head, embraced him, and the Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, said: 'Husayn is from me and I am from Husayn. May Allah love whoever loves Husayn. Al-Husayn is a grandson among grandsons (a tribe among tribes).'"

**Ruling:**

> **Hasan (Fair/Good)**

Abu Hurayrah, may Allah be pleased with him

**Core Text:**

> "I have never ceased to love this man after seeing the Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, do what he did. I saw Al-Hasan in the lap of the Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, putting his fingers into the beard of the Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, while the Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, was putting his tongue into his mouth. Then he said: 'O Allah, indeed I love him, so love him.'"

**Author's Comment:**

> "This is a hadith with an authentic chain of transmission (_sahih al-isnad_), but they [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] did not record it."

[Al Kafi Book 4 Ch 52 H#3](https://thaqalayn.net/hadith/1/4/52/3)

The above hadith tells us how significant our Muhammad ﷺ is in this world. We must obey what he says and must forbid ourselves from doing what he prohibits.

We can say that Muhammad ﷺ is the incharge of all creations.

The Quranic Verse used in the hadith is [Quran 59:7](https://quran.com/59/7)

Umm Salamah, may Allah be pleased with her

**Core Text:**

> "By the One whom I swear by, Ali was certainly the closest of people to the Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, at the time of his passing. We visited the Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, one morning while he kept saying repeatedly: 'Has Ali come? Has Ali come?' So Fatima, may Allah be pleased with her, said: 'It seems you have sent him on an errand.'
> 
> She said: Then Ali came afterward. Umm Salamah said: So I thought that he [the Prophet] had some private business with him, so we left the house and sat near the door, and I was the closest of them to the door. The Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, leaned over him and began whispering to him and conversing with him in private. Then the Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, passed away on that very day, so Ali was the closest of people to him at the time of his passing."

**Author's Comment:**

> "This is a hadith with an authentic chain of transmission (_sahih al-isnad_), but they [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] did not record it."

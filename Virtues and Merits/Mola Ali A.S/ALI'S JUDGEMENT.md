**Narrated by Ali, may Allah be pleased with him:**

> "The Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, sent me to Yemen. I said: 'O Messenger of Allah, I am a young man, and judicial cases are brought before me concerning which I have no knowledge.' He said: Then he placed his hand upon my chest and said: 'O Allah, make his tongue firm and guide his heart.' Since then, I have never doubted a judgment afterward."
> 
> **Author's Comment:** This is an authentic (_sahih_) hadith according to the criteria of the two Sheikhs [Al-Bukhari and Muslim], but they did not record it.

Al-Qadi Muhammad bin Umar bin Muhammad bin Salim ibn al-Baraa al-Hafiz al-Baghdadi, may Allah be pleased with him, narrated to us, saying: Al-Hasan bin Abdillah bin Muhammad bin Ali bin al-Abbas al-Razi narrated to us, saying: My father narrated to me that his master Ali bin Musa al-Rida narrated to me, from his father, from Ja'far bin Muhammad, from his father, from Ali bin al-Husayn, from his father al-Husayn bin Ali, from Ali, peace be upon him, who said: The Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him and his family, said:

**"Paradise longs for you (Ali), and for Ammar, Salman, Abu Dharr, and Al-Miqdad."**

> **Footnote Comment (1):** The hadith is widespread (_mustafid_) among both the specific group [Shia] and the general public [Sunni].

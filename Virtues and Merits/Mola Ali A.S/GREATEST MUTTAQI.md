Al-Sha'bi (reported as a historical narration regarding Ali, may Allah be pleased with him)

**Core Text:**

> "When Ibn Muljam struck Ali, may Allah be pleased with him, that strike, Ali advised concerning him, saying: 'Be gentle with him, treat his lodging kindly, and be good to him. If I survive, it is up to me to either forgive him or take legal retribution (_qisas_). If I die, execute him as retribution, but do not exceed limits; for indeed, I will argue my case against him before my Lord, the Mighty and Majestic.'"


[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4691](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4691)

![[image-98.webp|418x601]]![[image-96.webp|418x601]]

"Hazrat Abu Saeed Khudri (may Allah be pleased with him) said: A complaint was made to Hazrat Ali (may Allah be pleased with him) in the presence of Mustafa (peace be upon him), so he delivered a sermon and said: O people! Do not complain about Ali. By Allah, he is very strict about the essence of Allah and Jihad."

"This hadith is authentic with an authentic chain of transmission, but Imam Bukhari (may Allah have mercy on him) and Imam Muslim (may Allah have mercy on him) did not narrate it."

[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4654](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4654)

Ali is the most fearful in front of Allah.

"Hazrat Jabir (may Allah be pleased with him) said: The Messenger of Allah (peace and blessings of Allah be upon him) took hold of the arm of Hazrat Ali bin Abi Talib (may Allah be pleased with him) and said: This is the leader of the righteous, the killer of the wicked. Whoever helps them will be helped (by Allah), and whoever oppresses them will be humiliated (by Allah). His voice became louder as he said this."

This hadith has a sahih chain of transmission, but the two Sheikhs (may Allah have mercy on them) did not narrate it."

[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4644](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4644)


Ali is the Imam of the righteous.
Whoever teases Ali teases Aqa Muhammad ﷺ.

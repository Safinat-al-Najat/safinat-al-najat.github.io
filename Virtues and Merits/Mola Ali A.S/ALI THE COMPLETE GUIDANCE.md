Zayd bin Arqam, may Allah be pleased with him

**Core Text:**

> The Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, said:  
> "Whoever wishes to live my life, die my death, and dwell in the Eternal Paradise which my Lord has promised me, let him take Ali ibn Abi Talib as his ally (_wali_), for he will never take you out of guidance, nor will he ever enter you into misguidance."

**Author's Comment:**

> "This is a hadith with an authentic chain of transmission (_sahih al-isnad_), but they [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] did not record it."


[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4642](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4642)

Ali will never let you go astray.

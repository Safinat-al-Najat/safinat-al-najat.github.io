Ali, may Allah be pleased with him (reported by Rabi'ah bin Najid)

**Core Text:**

> "The Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, called me and said: 'O Ali, indeed there is an analogy between you and Jesus, peace be upon him. The Jews hated him so much that they slandered his mother, and the Christians loved him so much that they elevated him to a status that was not his.'
> 
> Ali then said: 'Behold! Two types of people will perish concerning me: an excessive lover who praises me for what I do not possess, and a malicious hater whose animosity leads him to slander me. Behold, I am not a prophet, and no revelation is sent to me. However, I act according to the Book of Allah and the Sunnah of His Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, as much as I am able. Therefore, whatever I command you regarding obedience to Allah the Almighty, it is your duty to obey me, whether you like it or dislike it. But if I command you to disobey Allah, then there is no obedience to anyone—including myself—in disobedience to Allah the Mighty and Majestic. Obedience is only in what is right and good.'"



[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4622](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4622)

It means one group will hate Ali too much and will be doomed and the people who will try to prove Ali more than he was will also be doomed.

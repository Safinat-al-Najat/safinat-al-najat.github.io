Al-Hasan bin Ali (reported by Al-Hurayth bin Mukhashshi)

**Core Text:**

> "Ali was killed on the morning of the twenty-first of Ramadan. [Al-Hurayth] said: I heard Al-Hasan bin Ali saying while he was delivering a sermon and mentioning the virtues of Ali: 'He was killed on the night the Quran was revealed, the night Jesus was taken up, and the night Moses passed away.' He said: And Al-Hasan bin Ali, peace be upon them both, led the funeral prayer over him."

**Author's Comment:**

> "This is a hadith with an authentic chain of transmission (_sahih al-isnad_), but they [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] did not record it."

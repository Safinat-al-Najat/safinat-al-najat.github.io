Anas narrated that the Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, said:

> "Paradise longs for three individuals: Ali, Ammar, and Salman."
> 
> **Author's Comment:** This is a hadith with an authentic chain of transmission (sahih al-isnad), but they [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] did not record it.

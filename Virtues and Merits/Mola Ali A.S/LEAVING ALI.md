The Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, said:

> "O Ali, whoever separates from me has separated from Allah, and whoever separates from you, O Ali, has separated from me."
> 
> **Author's Comment:** This is a hadith with an authentic chain of transmission (sahih al-isnad), but they [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] did not record it.

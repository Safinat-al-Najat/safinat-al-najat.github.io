![[Combined Virtues of Ali.png]]

[Jami at Tirmidhi 3724](https://sunnah.com/tirmidhi:3724)

Main points are:

1. Ali is like Harun PBUH of Aqa Muhammad ﷺ as Harun PBUH was for Moses PBUH. But there won't be any Prophet after Aqa Muhammad ﷺ.
2. Ali was the one who got banner of victory on the day of Khaibar
3. Ali was included in the family of Aqa Muhammad ﷺ along with Fatimah, Hassan and Hussain.

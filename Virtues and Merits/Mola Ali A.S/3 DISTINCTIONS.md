The Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, said:

> "Three things were revealed to me concerning Ali: that he is the master of the Muslims, the leader of the God-fearing, and the commander of the radiant ones."
> 
> **Author's Comment:** This is a hadith with an authentic chain of transmission (sahih al-isnad), but they [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] did not record it.

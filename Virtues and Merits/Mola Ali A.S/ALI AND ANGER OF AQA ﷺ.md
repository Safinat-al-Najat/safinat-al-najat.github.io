"It was narrated from Aisha (may Allah be pleased with her) that when the Prophet (peace and blessings of Allah be upon him) was angry, no one would dare to speak to him except Ali (may Allah be pleased with him). 

*This hadith has a sahih chain of transmission, but the two sheikhs (may Allah have mercy on them) did not narrate it."

[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4647](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4647)

So whenever Aqa Muhammad ﷺ was angry Ali used to speak to him.

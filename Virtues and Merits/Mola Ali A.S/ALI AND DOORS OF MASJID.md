Zayd bin Arqam, may Allah be pleased with him

**Core Text:**

> "Some of the companions of the Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, had doors opening into the mosque. One day he said: 'Close these doors except the door of Ali.' He [Zayd] said: So some people spoke about that. Then the Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, stood up, praised Allah and extolled Him, and then said: 'As for what follows: Indeed, I was commanded to close these doors except the door of Ali, and some of you have spoken regarding it. By Allah, I did not close anything nor did I open it, but rather I was commanded with something and I followed it.'"

**Author's Comment:**

> "This is a hadith with an authentic chain of transmission (_sahih al-isnad_), but they [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] did not record it."

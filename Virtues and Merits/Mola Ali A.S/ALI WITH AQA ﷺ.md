Qutham bin al-Abbas (reported via Abu Ishaq)

**Core Text:**

> Abu Ishaq said: "I asked Qutham bin al-Abbas: 'How did Ali inherit from the Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, rather than you [the rest of the family]?' He replied: 'Because he was the first of us to catch up to him and the closest of us to him.'"

**Author's Comment:**

> "This is a hadith with an authentic chain of transmission (_sahih al-isnad_), but they [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] did not record it."

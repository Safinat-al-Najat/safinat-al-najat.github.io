"Hadrat Abu Sa'id al-Khudri (RA) narrates: We were with the Messenger of Allah ﷺ when the strap of his sandal broke. Hadrat Ali (RA) stayed behind to repair it. The Prophet ﷺ walked a little further and then said: 'Indeed, there is one among you who will fight for the interpretation (_tawil_) of the Qur'an just as I fought for its revelation.'

People raised their heads (in curiosity); among them were Hadrat Abu Bakr (RA) and Hadrat Umar (RA). Abu Bakr (RA) asked, 'Is it me?' The Prophet ﷺ said, 'No.' Then Umar (RA) asked, 'Is it me?' The Prophet ﷺ said, 'No; rather, it is the one who is repairing the sandal (meaning Ali).'

We went to Hadrat Ali (RA) to give him this glad tidings, but he did not even lift his head, as if he had already heard this from the Messenger of Allah ﷺ before. 

(This Hadith is authentic according to the criteria of Imam Bukhari and Imam Muslim, though they did not record it)."

[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4621](https://quranohadith.com/mustadrak/4621)

**6937 - (Narrated Hadith):** Ahmad bin Ali bin al-Muthanna informed us, Uthman bin Abi Shaybah narrated to us, Jarir narrated to us, from al-A'mash, from Isma'il bin Raja, from his father, from Abu Sa'id al-Khudri, who said: I heard the Messenger of Allah, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, saying:

**(Text of the Hadith):** 'Indeed, there is one among you who will fight for the interpretation (Ta'wil) of the Qur'an just as I fought for its revelation (Tanzil).' Abu Bakr asked, 'Is it me, O Messenger of Allah?' He replied, 'No.' Umar asked, 'Is it me, O Messenger of Allah?' He replied, 'No, but rather it is the repairer of the sandal (Khasif al-Na'l).' And he had given his sandal to Ali to repair it."

"Its chain of narration is authentic according to the criteria of Imam Muslim"

[Sahih Ibn Hibban 6937](https://hadithunlocked.com/ibnhibban:6937)

> **11712 - Husayn bin Muhammad narrated to us, Fitr narrated to us, from Isma'il bin Raja al-Zubaydi, from his father, who said: I heard Abu Sa'id al-Khudri saying:**
> 
> "We were sitting down waiting for the Messenger of Allah ﷺ. He came out to us from one of his wives' rooms, so we stood up along with him. Then his sandal broke, and Ali stayed behind to repair it.
> 
> The Messenger of Allah ﷺ moved forward and we moved along with him. Then he stopped to wait for Ali, and we stopped with him. He ﷺ then said:
> 
> **'Indeed, among you is one who will fight for the interpretation (Ta'wil) of this Qur'an, just as I fought for its revelation (Tanzil).'**
> 
> We looked around expectantly, and among us were Abu Bakr and Umar. Abu Bakr asked, 'Is it me?' The Prophet ﷺ replied, **'No.'** Umar asked, 'Is it me?' The Prophet ﷺ replied, **'No, but rather it is the repairer of the sandal.'**
> 
> Abu Sa'id said: 'So we went to Ali to give him the glad tidings, and it was as if he had already heard it.'"


**It is graded as "إسناده حسن" (Its chain of narrators is Good/Hasan)**


[Musnad Ahmad 11773](https://al-hadees.com/musnad-ahmed/11773)

Ali fought for interpretation of Quran.

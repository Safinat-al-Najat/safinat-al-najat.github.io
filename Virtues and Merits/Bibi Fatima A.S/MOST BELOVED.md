"Jami' bin 'Umair narrated: I went with my mother to the house of Umm Al-Mu'minin, Hazrat 'Aisha. I heard her voice from behind the curtain. My mother was asking her about Hazrat 'Ali, upon him be peace. So Umm Al-Mu'minin said, "You are asking me about that man? By Allah! I do not know of any person other than Hadhrat 'Ali whom the Messenger of Allah loved more than him, and I do not know of any woman on the face of the earth whom he loved more than his wife (Fatima)."

This Hadith has a sound chain of narration (Sahih al-Isnad), but Imam Bukhari and Imam Muslim did not include it.

[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4731](https://al-hadees.com/mustadrak/4731)

Hadith itself is enough and doesn't needs any explanation.



![[image-137.png]]

"Hazrat Jami bin Umair (RA) narrates: I went with my aunt to the Mother of the Believers, Hazrat Aisha (RA). I asked her: Who was most beloved to the Messenger of Allah (PBUH)? She replied: Fatima (RA). I asked her: Among men? She said: Her husband (Ali). I found them (Fatima and Ali) to be frequent fasters and night worshipers."

This Hadith has a sound chain of narration (Sahih al-Isnad), but Imam Bukhari and Imam Muslim did not include it.

[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4744](https://al-hadees.com/mustadrak/4744)

Bibi Fatima was most beloved to Aqa Muhammad ﷺ.

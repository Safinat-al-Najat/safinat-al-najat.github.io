"Hazrat Ali (May Allah be pleased with him) narrates: The Holy Prophet (Peace and blessings of Allah be upon him) said: On the Day of Judgment, an announcement will be made: 'O people of the gathering! Lower your gaze so that Fatima, the daughter of the Messenger of Allah (Peace and blessings of Allah be upon him), may pass.' Consequently, she will pass while wearing two green cloaks.

Abu Muslim says: Abu Qilaba told me that Abdul Hamid says: (In this Hadith, instead of green) two red cloaks are mentioned.  

☆☆ This Hadith has an authentic chain of narrators (Sahih al-Isnad), but Imam Bukhari (RA) and Imam Muslim (RA) have not included it (in their collections)."

[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4757](https://al-hadees.com/mustadrak/4757)

![[image-261.png]]![[image-262.png]]
"Hazrat Ali (Radi Allahu Anhu) narrates that the Messenger of Allah (Sallallahu Alaihi Wasallam) said: On the Day of Judgment, an announcer will call out from behind a veil: 'O people of the gathering! Lower your gaze (out of respect) so that Fatimah, the daughter of Muhammad (the veiled one), may pass through.'

☆☆ This Hadith meets the criteria of Imam Bukhari (Rahmatullah Alayh) and Imam Muslim (Rahmatullah Alayh), though they did not narrate it in their own collections."

[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4728](https://al-hadees.com/mustadrak/4728)

The hadith shows that how much there is respect of Bibi Fatima even in Akhirah.

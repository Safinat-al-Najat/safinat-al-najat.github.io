**H#1:**

“God’s Prophet ﷺ drew four lines on the ground and said,‘Do you understand what this means?’ The people said, ‘No. God and God’s Prophet ﷺ know best.’ Then God’s Prophet ﷺ said, ‘The four best women in Paradise are the following: Khadijah - the daughter of Khuwaylid ; Fatimah - the daughter of Muhammad; Mary - the daughter of Imran; and Asia - the daughter of Muzahim and the wife of Pharaoh.”

**H#2:**

“God’s Prophet (MGB) drew four lines on the ground and said, ‘The four best women in Paradise are the following: Mary - the daughter of Imran; Khadijah - the daughter of Khuwaylid ; Fatimah - the daughter of Muhammad; and Asia - the daughter of Muzahim and the wife of Pharaoh.”

[Al Khisal Vol 1 Book 5 Ch 14](https://thaqalayn.net/chapter/10/5/14)
These 4 are the best woman of their time.

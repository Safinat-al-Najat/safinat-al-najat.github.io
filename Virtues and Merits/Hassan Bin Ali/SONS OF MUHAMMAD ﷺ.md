"**Hazrat Salman (may Allah be pleased with him) narrates: The Messenger of Allah (peace and blessings of Allah be upon him) said: 'Hassan and Hussein are my sons; whoever loves them, loves me, and whoever hates them, hates me, and whoever hates them, Allah will enter him into Hell."**

"This Hadith is authentic according to the standards of Imam Bukhari (may Allah have mercy on him) and Imam Muslim (may Allah have mercy on him), but they did not include it in their collections.**"

"Hazrat Jabir (may Allah be pleased with him) narrates that the Messenger of Allah (peace and blessings be upon him) said: **'The children of every woman are attributed to the father's side, except for the two sons of Fatima; for I am their guardian and they are attributed to me.'**

This Hadith has an authentic chain of narration (Sahih al-Isnad), but Imam Bukhari and Imam Muslim did not record it in their Sahih collections."

[Mustadrak Al Hakim 4770](https://al-hadees.com/mustadrak/4770)

According to this hadith, Hassan and Hussain are the sons of  Muhammad ﷺ.
